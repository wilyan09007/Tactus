▶ Reconciliation (source of truth: every disputed number re-derived) ▶ Methodology + literature review (GuitarSet / TapToTab / FretNet)
Rigor. One player → stratified k-fold + GroupKFold-by-run (never LOPO; stated). All preprocessing fit on train folds only; natural base rates + chance beside every number. The segmenter was the linchpin (a blind onset_detect over-fired 5×); we anchored it and audited it. We reconciled two independent analyses and adopted the value that reproduces, not the larger one — correcting an inflated E1 0.80→0.62, an inflated chord-ID 0.81→0.02, and a survivorship 93%→84% fret. We falsified our own bets where the data said so (vision position, chord-ID). The single honest ceiling is one player; a second hand is the next unlock. That is why the numbers are believable.
